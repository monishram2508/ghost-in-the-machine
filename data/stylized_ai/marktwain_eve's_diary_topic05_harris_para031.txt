The man truly believed he could charm the warts off a toad with a kind word and a pocketful of spare change. It's a quality, mind you, that's both endearing and alarming, like a newborn kitten armed with a blunderbuss.

He's got a habit of embellishing, too, our Harris. Not in a malicious way, understand, but in the manner of a painter who's just a tad too enthusiastic with the palette. A simple fishing trip, for instance, blossoms into a yarn involving a monstrous pike, a near-death experience wrestling with the beast in a leaky rowboat, and a rescue by a passing flock of trained seagulls.
